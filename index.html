Assault Rifle M16
The ARX-9 is a modern, modular assault rifle engineered for versatility and performance in diverse combat environments. Designed with precision, low recoil, and tactical adaptability, it delivers reliable firepower for both close-quarters and mid-range engagements.
  • Caliber : 5.56x45mm NATO
  • Action : Gas-operated, rotating bolt
  • Rate of Fire : 700-950 rounds per minute
  • Effective Range : 500 meters
  • Barrel Length : 14.5 inches (368 mm)
  • Overall Length : 33 inches (838 mm)
